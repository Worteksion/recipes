Ingredients:
- 400g minced meat
- 1/2 - 1 onion
- 1 clove of garlic
- 1 can of tomato sauce
- spices:
  - salt
  - pepper powder
  - chili powder
  - paprika powder
- spaghetti

Directions:

1, Slice the onion and garlic clove and saute them on the pan.
2, Add the minced meat and once the meat is cooked add the tomato sauce.
3, Simmer on low heat for 10 minutes.
4, Add spices (pepper, chili, paprika), mix them together and turn off heat.
5, Boil water in a pot and cook the spaghetti for 8-10 minutes.
6, Serve as soon as the spaghetti is ready.
